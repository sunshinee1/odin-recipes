Ingredients

  • Japanese short-grain rice (e.g. Koshikari)
  • Nori seaweed
  • Additional filling (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice in an instant pot, rice cooker, or donabe.
  2. Mold the rice into your preferred shape. You can use your hands or your tool of choice. Don't forget to salt the rice for food safety. Add fillings optionally.
  3. Wrap the rice ball in nori seaweed.
